Publications

  • Jinbiao Chen, Huiyu Zhang, Qingxin Chen, at al. 2020. Performance analysis of kanban controlled systems with resource sharing. Working in progress, planned to submit to International Journal of Production Research.

    • The first to research kanban controlled systems with resource sharing with general service times
    • Proposed new approximations based on queuing theory to evaluate performance measures of synchronization stations with resource sharing
    • Developed an iterative algorithm based on parametric decomposition and two-moment approximations to estimate the system performance

  • Jinbiao Chen, Huiyu Zhang, Ning Mao, at al. 2019. Modeling and analysis of a manufacturing cell with an automatic loading/unloading and transporting robot.Industrial Engineering Journal.

    • The first to analyse a stochastic manufacturing cell with a hybrid robot
    • Evaluated the system performance based on a Continuous Time Markov Chain model
    • Analysed the influence of stochastic factors on the system performance

  • Jinbiao Chen, Qingxin Chen, Ning Mao, at al. 2019. Layout optimization of a manufacturing cell with testing process.Industrial Engineering Journal.

    • The first to consider the layout problem of a stochastic manufacturing cell with a testing process
    • Found an optimal layout based on the performance evaluation by simulation
    • Analysed the effect of various testing rates on the optimal layout
